Understanding Search Methods

Semantic Search

Uses neural embeddings to understand meaning and context. Great for conceptual queries and finding semantically similar content even when exact words don't match.

  • ✓ Understands context and meaning
  • ✓ Handles synonyms and related concepts
  • ✗ May miss exact term matches
  • ✗ Computationally intensive

TF-IDF Search

Classical approach using term frequency and document frequency. Excellent for exact term matching and keyword-based searches.

  • ✓ Fast and efficient
  • ✓ Great for exact term matches
  • ✓ Interpretable results
  • ✗ Doesn't understand context

Hybrid Search

Combines the best of both worlds by merging semantic understanding with precise term matching for optimal results.

  • ✓ Balances precision and recall
  • ✓ Adaptable weighting
  • ✓ Robust performance
  • ✗ More complex to tune
